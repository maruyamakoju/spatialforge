Six Endpoints. Infinite Possibilities.

Everything you need to add spatial intelligence to your app, from depth estimation to floor plan generation.

📏 POST /v1/depth

Depth Estimation

Generate metric or relative depth maps from any single image. 16-bit PNG, NumPy, or colorized output. Sub-second inference on GPU.

📷 POST /v1/pose

Camera Pose

Estimate camera extrinsics from video frames. Get 6-DoF pose matrices for AR, VR, and SLAM applications.

📐 POST /v1/measure

3D Measurement

Measure real-world distances between any two pixels. Uses metric depth + camera intrinsics for accurate results.

🏗 POST /v1/reconstruct

3D Reconstruction

Generate point clouds and 3D meshes from images or video. Export as PLY or OBJ for downstream processing.

🏠 POST /v1/floorplan

Floor Plans BETA

Automatically generate 2D floor plans from room photos. Detect walls, doors, and windows from depth data.

🧊 POST /v1/segment-3d

3D Segmentation BETA

Segment objects in 3D space. Combine depth estimation with semantic segmentation for spatial understanding.
